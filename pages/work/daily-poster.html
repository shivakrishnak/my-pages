Morning Launch Sequence (60 min before study)
☀️
5:45 — Wake. No phone. 500ml water.
Step outside for natural light. Rehydrate before caffeine.
📓
5:55 — 5-min Journal
① Gratitude (specific) ② Today's single most important outcome ③ Name one anxiety. Then review yesterday's 5 key learnings.
🧘
6:05 — 10-min breathwork
Box breathing 4-4-4-4. Count breaths to 10. Restart when lost. Activates PFC.
🚶
6:15 — 30-min brisk walk (no phone)
BDNF dose. Think loosely about today's hard problem. Don't force solutions.
🍳
6:45 — Protein breakfast + first coffee
25-30g protein. Complex carb. Fat. Coffee only after 90 min of waking.

Brain Fuel — Eat More Of These
🥚 Eggs (3 daily)
Choline → acetylcholine. Memory neurotransmitter. Don't skip yolk.
🫐 Blueberries / berries
Anthocyanins → blood flow to brain. Memory boost within hours.
🐟 Fatty fish 3–4x/wk
DHA omega-3 — structural fat in brain cell membranes.
🌰 Walnuts (handful)
ALA omega-3 + polyphenols. Reduces neuroinflammation.
🍫 Dark choc 70%+
Flavanols + cerebral blood flow. 1–2 squares pre-study.
🫙 Curd / yoghurt
Gut-brain axis. Probiotics reduce anxiety via vagus nerve.
🌿 Palak / methi daily
Vitamin K, folate, lutein — working memory and brain health.
🍵 Green tea
L-theanine + caffeine = calm focus. Alpha brainwave state.
Avoid — Hurts Cognitive Performance
🚫 White sugar / refined carbs
Glucose spike → crash in 60–90 min. Brain fog, irritability.
🚫 Heavy oily lunch
Diverts blood to digestion. Cognitive drop for 2–3 hours after.
🚫 Skipping meals
Brain glucose drops → fog, anxiety. No glucose storage in brain.
🚫 Late heavy dinner (9PM+)
Raises core temp at wrong time. Fragments sleep quality.
Memory Techniques — Use These Every Day
1
Active Recall — close material, explain from memory first
2–3x better retention than re-reading. Always test before checking.
2
Spaced Repetition — Day 1 → 3 → 7 → 14 → 30
Review yesterday's 5 key points every morning. Use Anki.
3
Feynman — explain it as if teaching a 12-year-old
Where you stumble = your gap. Fill gap. Repeat until smooth.
4
Draw by hand — never just type complex concepts
Handwriting encodes deeper. Whiteboard prep built in.
5
Interleave subjects — Java 90m → System Design 90m → DSA
Harder but produces significantly better transfer and recall.
Mindset Anchors
"Motivation follows action — it doesn't precede it. Do the action first. The motivation comes after the first 5 minutes of starting. Count 5-4-3-2-1 and begin."
Mel Robbins · Neuroscience of habit formation

🫁 Anxiety spike? Double inhale through nose, long exhale out mouth. 2 reps. Works in 5 seconds. (Physiological sigh — Huberman Lab)

😰 Feeling stressed? Name it: "I feel anxious because ___." Labelling reduces amygdala activation immediately. Then ask: "What would I tell a friend feeling this?"

🔄 Missed yesterday? That's allowed. The rule: never miss twice. A 30-minute session counts. Progress over perfection is the mechanism, not the metaphor.

💪 Interview anxiety = "My body is preparing me. This arousal is energy I can direct." Stress reframed as fuel measurably improves performance. (Kelly McGonigal, Stanford)
