8 · Numbered Steps
  1. Prepare the context — Read the section once without taking notes. Let the ideas land.
  2. Retrieval practice — Close the page and write down everything you remember.
  3. Compare and correct — Open the page, find the gaps, mark what you missed.
  4. Apply it today — Find one real situation to apply the concept before end of day.
9 · Drill Card (Exercise)
Active Listening Sprint 5 min

In your next conversation, resist the urge to respond for the first 30 seconds. Take a mental note of one thing the speaker said that surprised you, then reference it when you do respond.

Days 31–60 · Depth — Layer deliberate practice on top of the foundation. Increase intensity by 20%.
Days 61–90 · Integration — Merge skills, review learnings, set the next cycle's direction.
