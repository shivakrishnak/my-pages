05Full Card-by-Card Reference

Complete benefit breakdown for every card, for whenever you need the details.

American Express Membership Rewards Credit Card (MRCC) — Opened 06/2023
  • Fees: Joining ₹1,000+GST, renewal ₹4,500+GST from year 2. Full waiver at ₹1.5L annual spend; 50% waiver between ₹90,000–₹1,49,999.
  • Base rewards: 1 MR point per ₹50 spent, excluding fuel, utilities, insurance, and cash transactions.
  • Milestones: 1,000 bonus MR points for 4 transactions of ₹1,500+ in a calendar month; another 1,000 bonus MR points for spending ₹20,000+ in a calendar month. Both need one-time enrollment.
  • Redemption: Best value via the 18K/24K Gold Collection (Amazon Pay, Flipkart, Myntra, Taj Hotel vouchers) or 1:1 transfer to Marriott Bonvoy. Avoid statement credit / pay-with-points — both undervalue points at ~25p each.
  • Lounge access: None.
  • Fuel: 0% convenience fee at HPCL pumps under ₹5,000; standard surcharges apply elsewhere.
  • Note: Points never expire.
Axis Bank Flipkart Credit Card — Opened 10/2020
  • Fees: ₹500+GST joining and annual. Waived on ₹3.5L+ annual spend (rent and wallet-load spends excluded from the waiver calculation).
  • Rewards: 5% cashback on Flipkart and Cleartrip, 7.5% on Myntra (each capped ~₹4,000/quarter), 4% on preferred partners (Swiggy, Uber, PVR, cult.fit), 1% on all other eligible spends.
  • Lounge access: Discontinued as of 20 June 2025 (previously 4 complimentary domestic visits/year).
  • Exclusions: No cashback on utilities, telecom, education, rent, insurance, government payments, fuel, wallet loading, gold/jewellery, EMI transactions, or Flipkart/Myntra gift cards.
  • Other: 1% fuel surcharge waiver (₹400–₹4,000 spend range, max ₹400/cycle). Dining Delights program via EazyDiner, up to 15% off (max ₹500/month).
Axis Bank SuperMoney RuPay Credit Card — Opened 11/2025
  • Fees: None — lifetime free, no joining or annual fee.
  • Structure: FD-backed (secured) card; credit limit is typically up to 90% of the linked fixed deposit.
  • Rewards: 3% cashback on UPI payments via the super.money app; 1% unlimited on all other eligible UPI and non-UPI spends. Since 1 Jan 2026, the 3% super.money cashback is capped to whatever you earn from other UPI/non-UPI spend in the same cycle (minimum ₹100 cap if other cashback is below ₹100).
  • Other: 1% fuel surcharge waiver (₹400–₹4,000, max ₹400/cycle). EDGE Reward Points also accrue on eligible transactions.
  • Note: Card access is invite-only through the super.money app.

06Adding a Future Card

This page is built to grow. When you get a new card (or close one), update it using this checklist so the comparison table and closure-review logic stay accurate:

  • Add a row to the At a Glance table with opened date, fee status, fee amount, waiver spend, and lounge access.
  • Add a full accordion entry in Full Card-by-Card Reference with the complete benefit breakdown.
  • If the card has an annual fee, add it to the Fee-Bearing Cards — Closure Review section with a recommendation, ranked by strength of the case to keep it.
  • If the card is lifetime free, add it to the Lifetime Free Cards grid instead — no closure review needed.
  • Re-check this page roughly every 6 months — issuers revise fee waivers and lounge terms often (as HDFC Regalia Gold and Axis Flipkart both did in 2025–26).
Verify before acting Card terms, fee waivers, and lounge policies shown here reflect publicly available information as of July 2026 and can change without much notice — this is especially true for Yes First Preferred, where the exact waiver threshold couldn't be confirmed with certainty. Before closing any card, log in to the issuer's app or call customer care to confirm current terms and check for any pending dues, reward points you'd forfeit, or impact to your credit utilization and credit history length.
